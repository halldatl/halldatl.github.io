Assignment 2 - Computer-aided design and cutting

Description

1. Use a CAD software to build a parametric design of a pressfit construction kit.
2. Cut your design with a laser cutter.
3. Document the process and upload your design files.

I started with sketching, on paper, a box and selected sizes for all dimensions.

I had to be very precise in all fitting dimensions, inside and outside, for the pressure-fit to work currectly.

I started my CAD-scetch using SolidWorks

  • Learn More about SolidWorks

I created parameters, Global Variables, under manage equations.

It was important that I had a parameter for the thickness of the material and for the thickness of the laser.

Then I drew each side of the cube using the parameters for equations.

It was important that I right placements for the thickness and laser parameters to maintain press-fit after scaling.

Then I scaled it down 3:10. And rearranged them for the best fit in the laser cutter.

I also had to save it to Adobe Illustrator (.ai file). Set Stroke to 0,2 and resize the Artboard to 60*30cm to create pdf for the laser cutter.

Then I cut it out with Epilog Laser mini.
